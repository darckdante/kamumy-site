Kamumy

A smile that softens mountains
Stubborn as a sunrise that won’t wait
Wrapped in dreams and silken sleep
Feet tapping to rhythms she’ll never admit
Grace with a hint of mischief
Eyes that hold a thousand untold tales
Elegance—whispered, not shouted
Kamum is not just a name—it’s a presence, a character dancing between dreams and reality. With a smile that disarms and a stubborn heart that charms, she is poetry in motion, even when at rest.
